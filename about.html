My Story

Why design? That's easy. It's in everything we do and is everywhere we go. Design shapes the world and the experiences we have in it. It's the sweet spot right at the intersection of problem solving and art. It requires passion, empathy, and an abundance of curiosity.


Beginning in middle school, I experimented within a variety of different design domains before uncovering my appetite for User Experience. I've been infected with the drive to make people's lives better, and design is my tool of choice. Sure, I create efficient, intuitive, and enjoyable interactions. But so do a lot of people. What distinguishes me from the pack is my hamster-wheel mind, constantly spinning, reimagining, and improving the experiences that shape our day-to-day.


I can wear a few different hats: designer, front-end developer, researcher. I like to get my hands dirty in the code. I can wireframe for days. Put quite simply, I love design.
