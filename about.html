My Story

Design can mean a lot of things, which is one of the reasons I love it. Design allows me to be creative. To solve problems. To connect with people and try to make their lives better. Design is limitless. It requires empathy, and passion. Through design, I not only shape the world around me, but continuously reshape my perspective, as well.


I first fell in love with design in the seventh grade. I spent my high school and early college years experimenting within different design domains, before finally finding my passion for UX. Studying design and working on design teams has taught me a lot. I have developed a drive to create intuitive, efficient, and beautiful experiences, with the user always at the center.


When I'm not nose-deep in my computer, you can find me hiking the beautiful trails of Northern California, streaming every song to exist on Spotify, scoping out the best sushi restaurants, or playing with my dogs.
