WORK ABOUT RESUME

Purpose - Enable IBM designers to work better with Agile methodology - Help Agile teams collaborate more efficiently - Develop a resource for designers to address their daily pain points in working within a cross-functional Agile team

Sprint 0: User Research User Interviews - across IBM CIO, with designers, Agile coaches, developers, and iteration managers Surveys - across IBM CIO and CIO Design Deliverables - user personas representing the users interviewed, comprehensive understanding of users' pain points.

Sprints 1 & 2: Ideation Design Thinking - As-Is Scenario Maps, Needs/Benefits Statements, Prioritization Grids, Big Idea Vignettes & other Design Thinking activities to better understand what we are trying to create and why.

Sprint 2: Release Roadmap Deliverables - Outlining the first-release MVP (minimum viable product) and establishing the resource information architecture.

Sprint 3: Content Creation Deliverables - Content for each module gathered via user interviews, literature reviews, and outside research on agile and design.

Sprint 4: Wireframing & Prototyping Deliverables - clickable Invision prototype, able to be user-tested, that is representative of the interactive experience we plan to build.

Sprint 5: High-Fidelity Mock-ups Deliverables - mock-ups of the visual design for the home page and Modules 1-3 & a comprehensive style guide detailing our color scheme, illustration style, and content theme.
