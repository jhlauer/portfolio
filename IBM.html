Context

Timeline

12 weeks

Team

4 Designers, 1 Researcher, 1 Project Manager

Role

UX Design Intern

Problem

How can we enable IBM designers to work better with Agile methodology?

Sprint 0: Discovery

User Interviews - across IBM CIO, with designers, Agile coaches, developers, and iteration managers


Surveys - across IBM CIO and CIO Design


Sprints 1 & 2: Ideation

Design Thinking - As-Is Scenario Maps, Needs/Benefits Statements, Prioritization Grids, Big Idea Vignettes & other Design Thinking activities to better understand what we are trying to create and why.

Sprint 2: Roadmapping

Deliverables - Outlining the first-release MVP (minimum viable product) and establishing the resource information architecture.

Sprint 3: Content Creation

Deliverables - Content for each module gathered via user interviews, literature reviews, and outside research on agile and design.

Sprints 4 & 5: Prototyping

Deliverables - clickable Invision prototype, able to be user-tested, that is representative of the interactive experience we plan to build; mock-ups of the visual design for the home page and Modules 1-3 & a comprehensive style guide detailing our color scheme, illustration style, and content theme.
